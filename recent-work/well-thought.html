Tweet Thought Ranker is a Chrome extension that leverages AI to rank tweets on a scale of 0 to 10 based on their creativity, uniqueness, reflectiveness, thoughtfulness, etc. The main idea behind is to increase your browsing experience by focusing on more meaningful content.

Key Features

  • Automated Tweet Ranking: Leveraging the free-to-use Google's Gemini-1.5-flash, this extension evaluates tweets on a scale of 0-10, considering factors such as creativity, uniqueness, reflectiveness, and thoughtfulness.
  • Personalized Timeline Management: Easily filter out engagement farming and low-quality tweets by hiding posts bellow a specified ranking.

Technologies Used

  • ChatGPT
